HoneyBunCake

Savory and sweet breakfast cake filled with delicious cinnamon swirl and covered in frosting

Ingredients:

  • 1 package yellow cake mix (18.25oz)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 8oz sour cream
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 container frosting

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 farenheit
  2. Combine ingredients 1-5 in a mixing bowl
  3. Pour half of batter in greased 13x9x2in baking pan
  4. Sprinkle half of sugar mixture over batter
  5. Repeat previous two steps
  6. Bake at 350 for 30 to 35 minutes or until a wooden toothpick inserted comes out clean
  7. Cover with frosting
