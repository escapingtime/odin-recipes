Shredded Taco Chicken

Tear apart slow cooker fajita chicken with peppers and onions

Chicken Ingredients:

  • 2 full chicken breasts, halved
  • 1 large pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • Taco seasoning packet
  • 2oz chicken broth

Taco Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of flour or corn tortillas
  • Shredded mexican blend cheese
  • Shredded romaine lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Queso

Directions:

  1. Turn on slow cooker to low setting.
  2. Place chicken and chicken broth in slow cooker. Cover with taco seasoning packet.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 5 hours.
  4. Add onions and peppers to slow cooker. Cover and cook for 1 hour.
  5. Wet a paper towel, wrap around tortillas, and heat in microwave for 30-45 seconds.
  6. Fill tacos as desired with chicken and toppings.
  7. Enjoy!
