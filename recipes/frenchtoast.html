French Toast

A delicous melt-in-your-mouth treat to start the day!

Ingredients:

  • 4 1-inch thick slices of Kings Hawaiian Bread
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp. cinnamon
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Maple Syrup

Directions:

  1. Slice bread into 1 inch thick sections.
  2. In a shallow mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinammon.
  3. Quickly dip slices (do not soak) in egg mixture and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Keep egg mixture stirred and spices well blended throughout dipping process.
  5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve with warm maple syrup.

Makes 4 servings.
